About Us

Lux Lab, LLC, founded by Bryan Taborga in 2018, is a unique hair salon located in Manassas, Virginia. The owner prides himself in his hair coloring abilities. In addition to coloring, the salon specializes in cutting both classic and contemporary styles while also offering treatments and haircare customized to customer needs. Bryan Taborga continues to educate himself on the latest techniques in order to give his customers exceptional experiences.
